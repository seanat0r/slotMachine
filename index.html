How does it Work?

Click on The button to start the spin. Each spin cost a minimum of 1 Coin and you bet a maximum of 20 coin. The more coin you set, more coins can you win. If you hit 0 Coin you lose. You can anytime reset with the refresh button underneath the spin button.
Beware that refreshing or any other activity that restart or refreshing this browser tap will delete your Coins and you need to start again!

Special Events

Pitty

With the pitty system it depends on your Bet, which pitty system its start to track. Importent is to know is, as soon you activate the other pitty system, the other will be destroyed and need to build from scratch.
The first pitty will track you lose streak, when you spend less than and same as 10 Coin. You need a lose streak of 5 to get the pitty system. You will lose the pitty as soon as you win or you spend the next to round higher then the pitty amount (=>10).
The second pitty system will function prety much the same but only you need to spen highher than 10 and it will activate by a lose streak of 3.

Win-Streak

As soon you win more than 3 in a row, every win will double the amount of coin you will get.

Score Event

At random score you will get diffrent Score-Event. It depends on how many games you played, your score amount and if you have luck to get one.
You can get diffrent rewards, the main reward is "Jackpot" wich you get a minimum of 100 coins!

Same Symbol Event

If you get three of the same Symbol, you might get a special reward.

Shortcut for keyboard

  • Enter = start a spin OR next
  • 0-9 = set your Coin
  • R = reset
